The 3-state model (now on all surfaces)

online — direct probe succeeded (healthy).
relay — direct probe failed but registry heartbeat fresh (≤300s); display-only, not counted online or work-targetable until relay transport is proven.
offline — direct probe failed AND no fresh heartbeat.

THREE render surfaces (web page / CLI / cockpit shell) derive this from the same healthy + reachable_via wire fields. The web side is now a single @/lib/fleetState helper used by both /fleet and /m/[id], so they cannot drift.
